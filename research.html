The variety of known marine invertebrates includes more than 230 thousand species. The actual diversity is several times greater and has not been precisely determined. Each expedition of marine biodiversity specialists brings dozens of new species. The biodiversity of the South China Sea, like other seas, remains understudied.

An integrative study of the diversity of marine invertebrates
in South China and other regions using original and collection material

  • New species for region and science
  • New data on morphology and evolution
  • Unexpected finds
